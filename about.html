About

Our Aim

The VEPC Snapshot aims to make core National Electricity Market data available quickly, completely and with high resolution. To achieve this we update the Snapshot daily and allow the user to download the entire history of half-hourly data from 1 January 2016. Our simple bar and line charts allow the user to easily investigate a specific time period through the use of a time navigator below the chart.
